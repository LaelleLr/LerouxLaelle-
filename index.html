About Me


Education


In my third year, I wanted to experience studying abroad and so took part in the Erasmus program at Copenhagen Business School, where I spent the semester majoring in Marketing. I followed this with a masters in Digital Marketing, Innovation and Start-up projects, studying in San Francisco. Most recently, I am undertaking a second masters in Web Management and Social Media, which involves an apprenticeship with L’Oréal in Paris.


Work experiences


Alongside my five years of studies, I have also taken part in four internships and one apprenticeship, which you can read more about on my LinkedIn profile. Currently, I am a digital content manager for the L'Oréal Group, having joined in January 2019. My rain role is to oversee the editorial calendar of loreal.com and loreal.fr, tasked with webmastering, SEO, following image rights, selecting and creating content according to the Corporate territories and coordinating the three main stakeholder contents (brands, countries and metiers). This apprenticeship has been a fantastic opportunity to develop my web management skills and of course, the thing I love the most about my job, I get to work with a wide variety of teams across the company.

The sunset mindset

      

I have always been more of a night owl than an early bird. This used to be something I wanted to change about myself but as I get older, I have learnt to embrace it, and the beauty of "evening energy". I like to call it the sunset mindset.

      

The idea is to see the evening as a window between the day and the night. A blissful moment to reflect on the day and to see all that the night has to offer. Just like the sunset, there is a beautiful transition between the day and the night; the sunset mindset is the ability to embrace what is gone and what is to come, with serenity and a fresh burst of energy.
